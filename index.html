Common Questions

Everything You
Need to Know

Why isn't shipping included in the product price? +
We show the exact factory cost price — zero markup. Shipping is calculated by the actual weight of your goods when they arrive. This means you never overpay or underpay. We send you the shipping company's pickup invoice, you pay them directly, and we collect your goods and deliver to you. This is the most transparent model possible.
How much will shipping cost me approximately? +
Rough estimates: Phone via Express Air ~₦15,000–₦40,000. Laptop ~₦25,000–₦55,000. Handbag via Standard Air ~₦8,000–₦20,000. Shoes ~₦10,000–₦22,000. These vary based on final package weight. Sea freight for bulk orders is significantly cheaper per kg. We always show you the exact amount before you need to pay anything extra.
Are all products 100% authentic? +
Absolutely. We source exclusively from certified manufacturers and authorized distributors. Every product arrives in original manufacturer packaging with intact seals and verifiable serial numbers. We have completed 200+ orders and have never sold a single counterfeit product. Our reputation is built entirely on this.
How does the online payment work? +
You add items to your cart, select your shipping method, fill in your details at checkout, and pay the product cost price via Flutterwave. Accepted: Nigerian debit/credit cards, bank transfer, USSD, mobile money. You receive an instant receipt to your email. Shipping is paid separately when your goods arrive — we send you the invoice at that point.
What if my item doesn't arrive or arrives damaged? +
All items are insured during transit. If anything goes wrong — which is extremely rare in our 200+ order history — we resolve it fully. Your payment is protected and we stand behind every order we process. Our customers' trust is the foundation of our business.
Can I order something not in the catalog? +
Yes — that's one of our core services! Use the Custom Request form or WhatsApp us directly. If you can find it online anywhere, we can almost certainly source and import it at cost price. Just share the product name, link, or full specifications and we'll send you a quote within 1–2 hours.
Is Port Harcourt delivery really free? +
Yes! Port Harcourt customers receive completely free local delivery after goods clear shipping from China. All other states pay a flat ₦5,000 delivery fee regardless of order size. Note: this is separate from the China-to-Nigeria shipping fee, which is weight-based and paid when goods arrive.
