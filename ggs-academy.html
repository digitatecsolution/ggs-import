About This Programme

Why Specialised Logistics Knowledge Matters

The international vehicle sourcing sector is one of the most structured, process-driven channels in global commerce. Navigating maritime freight, tracking international port movements, and understanding complex customs valuation metrics requires specific technical expertise.


This comprehensive video-based training programme is built strictly to break down these complex supply chain operations into clear, manageable educational modules — covering every stage from auction access through to final port clearance. Whether you are building institutional knowledge or entering the sector as a new practitioner, this course provides the procedural foundation you need to operate with confidence.

Core Curriculum

Four Technical Training Modules

Each module is structured around real-world supply chain procedures, with documented frameworks and step-by-step procedural walkthroughs.

Module 01

Auction Access Framework

Learn verified procedures to safely navigate international automotive bidding platforms and evaluate vehicle history documentation metrics. Covers platform architecture, bid sequence workflows, and documentation verification protocols.

Foundational
Module 02

Maritime Freight & Logistics

Understand container tracking systems, ocean transit management, and coordinating freight logistics across international ports. Covers Bill of Lading structures, port-to-port documentation, and transit timelines.

Operational
Module 03

Customs Valuation Navigation

A technical breakdown of domestic duty assessment frameworks, port documentation rules, and legal clearing procedures. Includes tariff schedule reading, valuation adjustment principles, and agent coordination workflows.

Regulatory
Module 04

Asset Inspection Standards

Methodologies for utilising verified electronic history databases to accurately evaluate mechanical condition metrics prior to acquisition. Covers VIN decoding, inspection report interpretation, and condition grading standards.
